Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

Overview

NodScroll is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Data Collection

NodScroll does NOT collect any data. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Store or upload camera images/video
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Share data with third parties

Camera Usage

NodScroll uses your camera for head gesture detection with the following principles:

  • Explicit Consent: Camera is only accessed when you explicitly enable tracking by clicking "Start"
  • Local Processing: All video processing happens locally in your browser using MediaPipe Face Mesh
  • No Storage: No images or video frames are saved, uploaded, or shared
  • Immediate Stop: Camera access stops immediately when you disable tracking or close the extension
  • Your Control: You can revoke camera permission at any time through browser settings

MediaPipe Face Mesh

NodScroll uses Google's MediaPipe Face Mesh library for face landmark detection. This library runs entirely in your browser (client-side) and does not send any data to Google or any external servers.

Data Storage

The only data stored locally on your device includes:

  • Your preference settings (sensitivity, scroll amount, language, etc.)
  • These settings are stored using Chrome's Storage API
  • No personal or identifying information is included

Permissions Explained

NodScroll requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: Required for head gesture detection
  • activeTab: Required to send scroll commands to the current tab
  • scripting: Required to inject content script for page scrolling
  • storage: Required to save your preference settings
  • offscreen: Required to run face detection in background

Third-Party Services

NodScroll does NOT use any third-party services for analytics, advertising, or data collection. The only third-party code is MediaPipe Face Mesh, which runs locally and sends no data externally.

Children's Privacy

NodScroll does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The extension can be used by anyone with parental consent where required.

Open Source

NodScroll is open source. You can review the complete source code at: https://github.com/hotea/NodScroll

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control by:

  • Disabling the extension at any time
  • Revoking camera permission through browser settings
  • Uninstalling the extension completely
